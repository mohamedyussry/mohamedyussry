My Professional Journey

My journey with remote work began in 2006 when I was just 16 years old, driven by my passion for technology. It all started with a website called "Geran" that allowed free page creation, and at the time, I didn't even know HTML.

Early Beginnings (2006-2010)

Websites were designed using tables, with no concept of responsive design. I was fascinated when I discovered codes, copying and experimenting to see the results - the beginning of my love for this field.

Forums and First Professional Experience (2010-2013)

I became active in forums like "Tradent", the largest community of developers and designers. I met my first employer, Abdullah Al-Ghamdi, who owned a voice chat website. My first paid task was performing basic SEO work like creating backlinks.

Professional Growth (2013-2019)

I received my first job offer from a company in Oman at age 21. My first major task was implementing an API integration with Omantel. I proposed adding website design services, which significantly improved the company's business.

Technology Leadership (2019-Present)

After working with various companies and managing multiple projects across Oman and Saudi Arabia, I now work from home in Egypt. My journey has been marked by continuous learning, innovation, and a persistent passion for technology.
